Background

Aang is the protagonist of the hit Nickelodeon series, Avatar: The Last Airbender. He is only Airbender and a monk of the Air Nomads. After reawakening from an iceberg for one hundred years, he masters bending the four elements: air, water, earth, and fire. With his flying bison, Apa, and his Water Tribe friends, Katara and Sokka, he goes on a quest to save the world from the Fire Nation.

Personality

Aang is a peace-maker and is usually reluctant to fight, unless he needs to protect others. He is playful, kind, and polite to friends and strangers, but stern when he faces his opponents.

Appearance

He is known for his shaved head and a light blue arrow extending from his back to his forehead. When he grows out his hair it is a dark brown color. He also has brown eyes. He wears an orange and red Air Nomad uniform.
